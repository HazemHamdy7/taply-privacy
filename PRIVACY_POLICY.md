# Privacy Policy - Taply

**Last updated: June 8, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Taply ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Business Card Data**: Name, phone number, email, company, job title, address, website, social media links, and any other information you choose to add to your digital business cards.
- **Profile Information**: Your name and photo if you choose to add them.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Analytics**: We collect local analytics data about how you interact with the App (e.g., number of cards created, cards viewed, shares). This data is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to our servers.

### Device Permissions
- **Camera**: Used for scanning QR codes on business cards. Camera access is optional and only used when you actively scan a QR code.
- **Storage**: Used to save business card images to your device's gallery.
- **NFC (Near Field Communication)**: Used to read and write business card information via NFC tags.

## How We Use Your Information

All data collected by the App is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not:

- Collect or transmit your data to any external server
- Use analytics services (Firebase, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your information
- Show ads

Your data remains on your device and is under your full control.

## Data Storage

- **Business Cards**: Stored locally using Hive database
- **Scanned Cards**: Stored locally using Hive database
- **Settings**: Stored locally using SharedPreferences
- **Analytics**: Stored locally using Hive database
- **Categories**: Stored locally using Hive database

## Third-Party Services

The App may open external applications via URL launchers (e.g., WhatsApp, Google Maps, phone dialer, email). When you tap on these links, you leave the App and the respective third-party's privacy policy applies.

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend using your device's built-in security features (PIN, password, biometric lock) to protect your data.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:
1. Deleting individual cards within the App
2. Clearing the App's data through your device settings
3. Uninstalling the App

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
