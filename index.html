Taply Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Taply respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, stored, and used within the application.

Information We Collect

Taply stores business card information created by users directly on the user's device. This may include:

  • Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Company Information
  • Social Media Links
  • Notes and Categories

Camera Permission

Camera access is used only for:

  • Scanning QR codes
  • Scanning business cards

No camera images are uploaded to external servers.

NFC Permission

NFC access is used only for:

  • Reading NFC business cards
  • Writing NFC business cards

Storage Permission

Storage access is used only for:

  • Saving exported card images
  • Saving backup files
  • Importing user-selected files

Contacts Permission

If granted by the user, contacts permission is used only to:

  • Save business card information into the device contacts

Taply does not upload contacts to any server.

Data Storage

Currently, all user data is stored locally on the user's device using local storage technologies. No cloud synchronization is enabled in the current version.

Analytics

Taply generates analytics locally on the user's device for features such as card views, QR scans, NFC opens, and networking insights. No analytics data is transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Taply may use platform services provided by:

  • Google Play Services
  • Android System Components
  • Apple iOS Components

Data Sharing

Taply does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Taply is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
